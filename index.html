Ambrosia Hawthorn

Ambrosia Hawthorn, a witch, author of The Spell Book for New Witches, Seasons of Wicca, and The Wiccan Book of Shadows, is also the founder of Witchology Magazine.

Today, Ambrosia lives in the Pacific Northwest, where she writes full time and is the editor of an online blog for modern Witchcraft.
